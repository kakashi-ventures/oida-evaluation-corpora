# Caso A — ClearPath Solutions Process Review

**Domain:** Consulting / Operational Process Optimization
**Primary evaluation corpus** referenced in the paper (Section 4.2, Brook vs. Cowork comparison).

## Scenario

ClearPath Solutions Inc. is a compliance documentation and regulatory management firm (78 employees, ~EUR 12M revenue) headquartered in Florence, Italy. Rapid growth has exposed structural weaknesses: slow onboarding (6 weeks), inconsistent client intake processes, audit workflow bottlenecks, and fragmented knowledge sharing.

An external consulting firm (Meridian Labs) is engaged to conduct a process review, identify operational bottlenecks, and deliver improvement recommendations.

## Epistemic Characteristics

This corpus is designed to contain:

- **Decisions** that evolve through the engagement lifecycle
- **Contradictions** between different teams' assessments of bottleneck severity
- **Open questions** about audit workflow efficiency that remain unresolved
- **Hypotheses** about root causes that are progressively confirmed or refuted
- **Observations** at varying levels of formality (field notes vs. formal reports)
