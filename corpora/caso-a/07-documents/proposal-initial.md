## I. Executive Summary

This proposal outlines Meridian Labs' understanding of the opportunities and challenges facing ClearPath Solutions Inc. and presents a structured approach to diagnose operational bottlenecks, design process improvements, and create a roadmap for sustainable growth.

ClearPath is positioned in an attractive market segment—compliance documentation and regulatory management services—at a moment of market expansion driven by regulatory proliferation (NIS2, DORA, CSRD implementation across the EU). The organization has demonstrated strong client retention and revenue stability, indicating quality delivery and customer satisfaction.

However, ClearPath's ability to capture growth is constrained by operational inefficiencies that limit scalability:

- **Lengthy onboarding processes** (6–8 weeks current vs. 3–4 weeks industry best practice) reducing team utilization and limiting project capacity
- **Audit bottlenecks** (5–8 days per audit request, inconsistent quality) creating client dissatisfaction and team frustration
- **Fragmented knowledge infrastructure** limiting team capability and creating dependency on individual experts
- **Inconsistent intake processes** creating rework and quality variance
- **Manual reporting and analytics** consuming time better spent on client delivery

The market opportunity is significant. ClearPath's stated growth target (150–200 employees within 3 years; ~€18M–€22M revenue) is achievable with structural process improvements that reduce delivery friction and enable scaling without proportional headcount increase.

**Meridian Labs proposes a 12-week engagement** to diagnose current-state operations, identify critical bottlenecks and root causes, design target-state processes, and create a prioritized implementation roadmap with effort and dependency estimates.

**Engagement scope:** €85,000 | **Timeline:** September 8 – November 28, 2025

## II. Situation Analysis and Opportunity

### Market Context

The European compliance documentation services market is growing 7–9% annually, driven by:

1. **Regulatory expansion:** NIS2 (essential entities), DORA (financial services), CSRD (sustainability), and AI Act creating new documentation demand among mid-market organizations
2. **Compliance talent scarcity:** Mid-market organizations unable to build internal capabilities; outsourcing increasingly preferred
3. **Technology adoption:** Organizations seeking digitized compliance workflows; automation and AI-assisted documentation emerging

ClearPath operates in an attractive market segment (mid-market clients, €5M–€500M revenue) with multiple growth tailwinds.

### ClearPath's Current Position

**Strengths:**
- Strong client retention and satisfaction (indicated by repeat business and referrals)
- Established market presence in Italian and Southern European markets
- Experienced team with deep regulatory expertise
- Stable revenue and healthy margins

**Constraints on growth:**
- Current operational model does not scale efficiently beyond ~100 employees
- High dependency on key individuals (subject matter experts) creating utilization constraints
- Manual, paper-intensive workflows limiting capacity and consistency
- Limited knowledge management infrastructure; expertise siloed
- Inconsistent intake and scoping processes creating rework
- Ad hoc analytics and reporting limiting management visibility and decision quality

### The Opportunity

With modest process optimization and modest technology augmentation, ClearPath can:

1. **Reduce delivery friction:** 6-week onboarding → 3-week onboarding (30% increase in available project hours)
2. **Improve consistency:** Standardized audit processes reducing rework and client dissatisfaction
3. **Enable expertise scaling:** Knowledge management infrastructure allowing junior staff to deliver quality work more independently
4. **Improve visibility:** Data-driven management enabling better resource planning and client profitability analysis
5. **Support growth:** Operational foundation for scaling from ~80 to 150+ employees

These improvements directly support ClearPath's stated strategic objectives and are achievable within 6–12 months of implementation.

## III. Proposed Approach

### Engagement Structure

**Phase 1: Diagnosis (Weeks 1–4)**

Meridian Labs will conduct a comprehensive diagnostic of ClearPath's current operations:

- **Process mapping:** Document end-to-end processes for onboarding, audit, reporting, and project delivery
- **Bottleneck identification:** Identify process steps consuming disproportionate time/effort or creating quality variance
- **Root cause analysis:** Understand why bottlenecks exist (capability gaps, tool limitations, structural issues, individual behaviors)
- **Team interviews:** Structured conversations with team members at all levels to understand workflow, pain points, and informal workarounds
- **Data analysis:** Review project intake, project hours, team utilization, and profitability data
- **Benchmarking:** Compare ClearPath's processes against industry best practices and peer firms

**Deliverable:** Diagnostic report with current-state process maps, bottleneck summary, and preliminary root cause hypotheses (Week 4)

**Phase 2: Design (Weeks 5–9)**

Based on diagnostic findings, Meridian Labs will design target-state processes and supporting infrastructure:

- **Process redesign:** Develop improved processes for onboarding, audit, reporting, and project delivery
- **Tool requirements:** Identify technology gaps and specify tools/platforms to support improved processes
- **Organizational design:** Assess team structure, skill requirements, and training needs to support new processes
- **Change management:** Develop change management approach and communication strategy
- **Pilot recommendations:** Identify quick wins and processes suitable for pilot testing

**Deliverable:** Design recommendations (Weeks 8–9), presented to leadership team

**Phase 3: Roadmap and Handoff (Weeks 10–12)**

Meridian Labs will develop a detailed implementation roadmap and prepare ClearPath to execute:

- **Prioritized roadmap:** Sequence recommendations by impact, effort, and dependencies
- **Effort and timing estimates:** Quantify effort, timeline, and resource requirements for each initiative
- **Implementation approach:** Define how each initiative will be executed (internal team, external partner, phased rollout, etc.)
- **Success metrics:** Define measures to track progress and impact (cycle time, quality, utilization, revenue per FTE, etc.)
- **Team capacity planning:** Assess internal capacity to execute roadmap; identify external support requirements
- **Knowledge transfer:** Ensure ClearPath leadership understands rationale, can communicate to team, and can adapt roadmap as needed

**Deliverable:** Final report with prioritized implementation roadmap (Week 12)

### Working Approach

- **Embedded team:** Meridian Labs will maintain on-site presence (Florence office) 3 days/week during Weeks 1–6; flexible scheduling thereafter
- **Collaborative process:** Diagnosis and design will be conducted in close collaboration with ClearPath leadership (CEO, Head of Operations, CTO) and broader team
- **Weekly sync:** Status meetings with leadership team every Friday during engagement
- **Interim presentations:** Mid-point presentation (Week 6) to ensure alignment and gather feedback
- **Transparent process:** ClearPath team will have visibility into all work products; open feedback channel throughout

### Scope and Limitations

**In scope:**
- Onboarding processes
- Audit and quality assurance processes
- Project intake and scoping
- Project management and delivery tracking
- Reporting and analytics
- Knowledge management infrastructure
- Organizational design and staffing requirements

**Out of scope:**
- Detailed financial modeling or pricing strategy optimization
- Market positioning or competitive strategy
- Recruitment or talent acquisition
- Legal or compliance advisory beyond process requirements
- Technology implementation (excluded; specification only)
