[10:10] David: Four weeks is still a big improvement.

[10:16] David: That requires training people on the new process. How long?

[10:17] Nora: Two to three weeks rollout. You'd start with one team as a pilot, validate the process, then scale. The assumption is that people understand the rationale and buy in. If there's resistance, it'll take longer.
