TEXT:
Marta,

Clara will bring the small parcel this afternoon: bread, honey, and the tincture Tommaso prepared. I told her to stay on the main road because Enrico mentioned fresh tracks near the birch crossing.

Please keep the latch closed until she arrives. She will knock three times.

Ada
