TEXT:
I was walking back from the chapel when I heard what sounded like Marta's voice from the direction of the Old Mill cottage. It must have been around a quarter to four, though I cannot be sure because the chapel bell had not rung correctly that day. The voice sounded strained, almost lower than usual. I did not go closer.
