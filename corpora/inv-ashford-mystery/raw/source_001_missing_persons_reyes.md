Missing persons report filed by Elena Reyes regarding her son Daniel Reyes, 19, of 1814 Pierce Ave. Last seen by family on the morning of July 12, 1988. Subject did not return home that night. Mother became concerned on the 13th when subject failed to pick up younger sister from summer school. Subject is 5'8", brown hair, brown eyes, was wearing a gray t-shirt and jeans. Works part-time at Klein's Supermarket. No history of running away. No known disputes. Mother states subject typically spent evenings at Empire Arcade on Mercer Street. Inquiry to be opened.
