"FOUR DEATHS HAUNT ASHFORD: Pattern Emerges?"
The recovery last week of Tyler Marsh, 20, from the grounds of the abandoned Atchison depot brings to four the number of unexplained deaths in Ashford in just over two years. Marsh joins Daniel Reyes (1988), Marcus Webb (1989), and Eric Halloran (1989) on a list that has begun to draw uncomfortable comparisons. Sources close to the investigation, speaking on condition of anonymity, point to a recurring description: a soft-spoken man in his late twenties or early thirties, wire-rimmed glasses, dark jacket. "If it isn't one person, it's an awful coincidence," one source said. Ashford PD spokesperson declined to comment on whether the cases are being investigated as connected.
