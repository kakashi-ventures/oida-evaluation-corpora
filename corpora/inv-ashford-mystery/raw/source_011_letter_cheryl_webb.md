Detective Reilly,
I keep thinking of the call. It was Wednesday the first of March, week before he went missing. He told me a guy had offered him fifty dollars to "pose for some pictures." He was laughing about it. I asked if it was a creep thing and he said no, the guy seemed normal, said he did portraits. Marcus said he wasn't going to do it but he was thinking about whether he could get more money. I didn't ask where he'd met the guy. I wish I had. He also said he was thinking about leaving Ashford in the summer. The work was hard and he hadn't made any friends. I should have made him come home in February when he asked about the bus fare.
Cheryl
