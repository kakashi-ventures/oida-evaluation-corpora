TEXT:
Body of an adult Black male recovered from drainage culvert running under Route 14, approximately 400 yards from the rail spur servicing the disused Pruitt grain elevator. Reported by motorist Sandra Vincelli who pulled over after seeing what she initially thought was a discarded mattress in the ditch. Body partially submerged in standing water. No wallet, no identification, no jewelry. Partially clothed; some garments missing. Recovered to morgue for identification. Fingerprints subsequently matched to Marcus Webb.
