TEXT:
Carmen,
I keep thinking about the camera. He had been saving for one all spring — a real one, not the disposable kind. He asked me for forty dollars in June and I told him I didn't have it. I keep thinking, if I had given him the forty, would he have stayed home that night to look at catalogs instead of going to the arcade. The detective asked if Danny had any new friends. He didn't, not that I knew. He hadn't been to the arcade for almost two months in the spring — he was working extra shifts — and then he started going again in June. I wonder now if someone had been waiting for him to come back.
Elena
