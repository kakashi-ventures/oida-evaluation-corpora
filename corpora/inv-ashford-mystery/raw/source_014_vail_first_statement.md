We were drinking. Yeah, we'd been fighting. The same thing. He wanted me out and I wasn't ready to go. I left around midnight, took the 27 bus across town to Doug's place. Doug Halbert. Stayed there until — I don't know, until it was getting light. Took the first bus back. When I came in he was already — he was like that. I didn't do this. I know how it looks. We'd been arguing for months. But I didn't do this. I left at midnight and he was alive.
