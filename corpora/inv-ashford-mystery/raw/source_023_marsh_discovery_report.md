TEXT:
Body recovered from drainage ditch on the southwest perimeter of the disused Atchison freight depot, approximately 600 yards from the active rail siding. Reported by deer hunters who entered the depot grounds and noted unusual scent and corvid activity. Adult white male, decomposition advanced. No identification on body, no wallet. Body subsequently identified via dental records and a small tattoo on the left shoulder ("M") as Tyler Marsh, missing since October 4, 1990. Posture and lividity inconsistent with recovery position; body was moved post-mortem.
