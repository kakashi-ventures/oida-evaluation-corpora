TEXT:
Subject identified via dental records as Daniel Reyes. Body recovered August 3 along Norfolk Southern right-of-way, mile 142.6. Decomposition consistent with approximately three weeks post-mortem; consistent with disappearance date of July 12-13. Toxicology positive for barbiturate metabolites, concentration consistent with overdose. No blunt force trauma. No sharp force trauma. No ligature marks. Cause of death: probable acute barbiturate intoxication. Manner: undetermined; circumstances suggestive of administration by another party. Lividity patterns inconsistent with recovery position — body was moved post-mortem. Insect activity at recovery site does not match a body that decomposed in situ for the full interval.
