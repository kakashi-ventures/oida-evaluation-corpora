TEXT:
The 47th Annual Ashford County Fair will be held October 12-15, 1990 at the fairgrounds on Route 7. Livestock auction Saturday at 10 a.m. The carnival midway returns this year after a one-year absence. Volunteer signups for ticket booth and parking are available at the courthouse, Room 104. Junior division entries (livestock, baking, and crafts) must be submitted by October 5. Adult division entries due October 8.
