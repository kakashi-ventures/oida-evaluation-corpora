TEXT:
Responded to call at 06:42, 18 December 1989. Apartment 3C, 412 Wendell. Decedent: Eric Halloran, 24, white male. Discovered by roommate Patrick Vail at approximately 06:20. Body supine in bed. Apparent cause of death ligature strangulation; electrical cord from bedside lamp wrapped twice around the neck. Lamp lying broken on the floor on the south side of the bed, base shattered. Defensive injuries on both forearms, abrasions consistent with grabbing or being grabbed. Bruising at the throat under the ligature includes thumb-shaped marks suggesting manual pressure preceded the ligature. Wallet on dresser contains $120 cash and credit cards; untouched. No drawers disturbed. Front door not forced; deadbolt unlocked from the inside. Rear window closed and locked. No defensive weapons recovered.
