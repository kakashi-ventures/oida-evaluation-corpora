TEXT:
Yeah, I remember the order because I had to get the bucket of ice from the back. Rum and coke, extra ice. Guy was specific about it. Soft voice, didn't have to raise it over the jukebox even though I almost couldn't hear him. Maybe eleven-thirty, quarter to twelve? Younger guy with him was quiet. Not drunk, not exactly, but slow — like he was already tired. Paid in cash, both drinks, and a five for me. I couldn't tell you what the older guy looked like to save my life. Average. Glasses maybe. The kind of face you forget by the time he's out the door.
