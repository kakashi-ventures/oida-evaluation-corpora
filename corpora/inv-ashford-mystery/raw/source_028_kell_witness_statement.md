TEXT:
After I read about the Halloran boy in the paper I remembered: I saw a man on Wendell that night. Tall. He had glasses. Walking along the sidewalk on the west side. It was the night of the seventeenth because I'd been at bingo and was walking back. He was just walking. I couldn't see his face. He had a dark coat or a jacket. He was tall. That's all I remember. Yes, the seventeenth, I'm sure. I always go to bingo on Tuesdays.
[Internal annotation, MS: St. Anselm's bingo records show Mrs. Kell present on December 12 and 14 of that week. The 17th was a Sunday; no bingo was held. Witness reliability noted as low.]
