TEXT:
Smelled it before I saw it. Body about twenty feet off the rail, in the weeds. Didn't go closer once I was sure. Called it in to dispatch. While I was waiting I noticed a camera case in the brush, maybe ten feet from the body — black, Polaroid type, the kind with the strap. Empty. No camera inside, no film. Didn't pick it up. Told the deputy when he arrived. I've walked that stretch twice a week for nine years and that case wasn't there last Tuesday.
