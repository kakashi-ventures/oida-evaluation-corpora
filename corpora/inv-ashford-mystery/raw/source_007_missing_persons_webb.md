Missing persons report regarding Marcus Webb, 22, Black male, 6'1", recently of 904 Halsted St., Apt 2. Reported by sister Cheryl Webb of Lexington, KY, who states last phone contact was March 8 evening. Subject relocated from Lexington in November 1988 for employment at Hartford Hotel laundry, night shift. Report filed nine days after last contact due to family assumption that subject had simply not called. Subject has no Ashford-area family. Coworker confirms subject did not show for March 9 or any subsequent shift. Employer assumed subject had quit without notice.
