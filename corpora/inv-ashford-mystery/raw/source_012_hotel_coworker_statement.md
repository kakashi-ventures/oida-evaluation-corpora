TEXT:
Marcus told me once that some guy had come up to him on his smoke break and asked if he did any modeling. He thought it was creepy but he was kind of flattered too. I never saw the guy myself — Marcus pointed at the alley but the guy was already gone by then. When was it? I don't know exactly. Two weeks before he disappeared? Three? Maybe more. We didn't talk about it again. Marcus was the kind who'd shrug something like that off and not mention it twice.
