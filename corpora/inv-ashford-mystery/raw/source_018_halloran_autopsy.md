Subject: Eric Halloran. Cause of death: asphyxiation by ligature strangulation. Ligature: braided electrical cord, consistent with the cord recovered from the broken bedside lamp at the scene. Additional findings: bruising at the throat under the ligature line, including thumb-shaped marks anterolaterally, indicating manual strangulation preceded ligature application. Defensive wounds on both forearms; one bitten fingertip on the left hand (the decedent's own bite, defensive). Toxicology: blood alcohol 0.09. No barbiturates, no opiates, no sedatives. Time of death window 12:30 to 03:00 on 18 December 1989. Pattern is consistent with a struggle of one to several minutes followed by finishing with the ligature. Attacker was within reach of the bedside lamp.
