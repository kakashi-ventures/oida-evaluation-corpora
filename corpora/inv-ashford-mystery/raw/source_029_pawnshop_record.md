Ledger entry, July 20, 1988. Item: Polaroid 600 Instant Camera, used, with strap. Sale to shop (not pawn): $18.00. Seller name as recorded: "James Conroy." ID presented: Ohio driver's license (photocopy taken). Seller description in margin: "tall, glasses, dark jacket, polite." (Note from Hennessey, 1991: when contacted about this entry following the Sentinel article, I checked the file. The photocopy of the Conroy license was lost in the office flood in spring 1991. I do recall the man — quiet, didn't haggle. The address on the license, when checked at the time, turned out to be a vacant lot in Toledo.)
