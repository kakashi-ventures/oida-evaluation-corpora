TEXT:
Follow-up on Webb tip ("fifty dollars to pose"): interviewed Larry Pelham, proprietor of Pelham Studio (Sixth Street). Pelham acknowledges occasionally hiring local models for catalog work and bridal portfolios; offers between $50-$100 per session. Provided client logs and tax records. On the date of Webb's disappearance (March 8-9), Pelham was photographing the Lavalle-Stuart wedding in Cincinnati, OH. Alibi corroborated by three members of the wedding party, timestamped Polaroid proof prints, and a Hampton Inn receipt. Cleared as suspect in Webb matter. No basis to connect to Reyes matter. Case file closed re: Pelham.
