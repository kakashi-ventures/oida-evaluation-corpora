They were at it again on the seventeenth. Started around eleven, maybe a little before. Same shouting they've been doing since the autumn — the one wants the other out, the other won't go. I heard the front door of their apartment slam around midnight. I assumed it was Patrick leaving — he's usually the one who storms out. After that I slept until almost four when my cat knocked something off the dresser. Building was quiet then. Nothing else until the police arrived in the morning.
