Patrick came over after midnight sometime. We had some beers. Put on a movie — the Schwarzenegger one, I forget which. He stayed maybe two hours. Left before the movie was over. I went to bed. No, I don't know what time exactly. Was he upset? Not really. Tired. He's always tired lately. We've been friends since high school. He's not — he wouldn't.
