## Slide 2: The Opportunity
Fashion e-commerce is a €127B EU market with:
- High return rates (25–40%, vs. 15% across e-commerce)
- Size mismatches driving 60% of returns
- Abandoned carts: 70% across fashion retail
- Personalization maturity still low compared to other verticals
- Merchants lack insight into browsing behavior & fit intent

**The Gap:** Luxury & fashion brands know SKU-level sales but not *why customers browse, hesitate, and leave*.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
DataPulse is a behavioral analytics layer purpose-built for fashion:

**Product Capabilities:**
- Real-time heatmaps of product page interactions (zoom, rotate, size chart clicks, reviews)
- Fit intent detection (ML model: "customer likely trying to determine size/fit")
- Abandonment prediction (session-level: which customers will bounce)
- Recommendation engine (contextual sizing, cross-sell based on browsing signals, not just purchase history)
- A/B testing module (size chart layout, image carousel, product description positioning)

[VISUAL: Dashboard showing product heatmap with color-coded interaction zones (size selector highlighted in red = high abandonment), fit intent confidence score (67%), abandoned cart funnel visualization]

---

## Slide 4: Why Fashion?
Fashion e-commerce has unique challenges:
- Fit is subjective & varies by brand, region, body type
- Visual-first shopping (consumers rely on images + reviews, not product descriptions)
- High variance in return motivation (fit, color, expectation mismatch)
- Regulatory tailwind: EU Digital Services Act pushing for transparency in recommendations

**Incumbents:** Generic analytics tools (GA4, Mixpanel) don't capture fit intent or size-specific abandonment.
