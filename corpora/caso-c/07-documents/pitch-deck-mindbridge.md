## Slide 2: The Problem
Employee turnover costs companies 30–50% of annual salary per lost employee:
- IT firms lose €80K–150K per engineer departure
- Training + onboarding + lost productivity costs billions in EU annually
- Managers lack early signals of disengagement
- Current tools (surveys, exit interviews) are reactive, not predictive

**Pain:** Preventable turnover + morale decline costs European companies €47B/year.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
MindBridge uses AI/ML on enterprise data to predict engagement risks:
- Analyzes calendar patterns (meeting load, collaboration), email sentiment, project churn, tenure tenure, role transitions
- Flags at-risk employees 6–8 weeks before resignation (typically 30–45 days notice)
- Recommends interventions (manager conversations, role adjustments, development plans)
- Integrates with Slack, email, calendar systems (Microsoft 365, Google Workspace)
- Privacy-first: anonymized, no individual message content stored

## Slide 6: Technology
**Data Sources:**
- Calendar availability & meeting patterns
- Email send/receive frequency, sentiment analysis
- Slack activity & channel participation
- Tenure, role changes, performance reviews (via HRIS API)

**Model:**
- Supervised learning on historical churn data
- Feature importance: meeting load (18%), email responsiveness decline (16%), role transition (14%), peer collaboration drop (13%)
- Current accuracy: 72% on pilot data (predicting 6-8 week horizon)

**Infrastructure:** Python/ML pipeline, REST API, web dashboard (React)

## Slide 7: Business Model
- **Per-Employee-Per-Month (PEPM):** €1.50–2.50 depending on org size
- **Minimum:** 100 employees → €150–250/month
- **Example:** 500-employee company → €750–1,250/month

**Rationale:** High ROI if prevents even 1 departure/year per 200 employees (breakeven). Payback < 2 months.
