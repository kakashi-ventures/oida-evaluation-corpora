# Vertex Minds LLC — Demo Day Alpha

## Overview

Demo Day Alpha is the inaugural pitch event organized by Vertex Minds LLC, scheduled for **November 12, 2025** at Palazzo delle Industrie, Milan. The event represents our first structured opportunity to deploy capital from Fund I and establish Vertex Minds as a credible early-stage investor in the Italian AI and deep-tech ecosystem.

## Format

- **5 startups** selected from a pipeline of 22 inbound applications and 8 proactive sourcing contacts
- Each startup delivers a **5-minute pitch** followed by **5 minutes of Q&A** with the investment committee
- Networking aperitif post-event (~90 minutes)
- Internal IC deliberation session scheduled for the following day

## Selection Process

The pipeline was filtered through four stages:

1. **Open call** (3 weeks, closed October 17): applications via website form + deck submission
2. **Deck screening** by Tomás Herrera: 30 applications → 11 shortlisted based on stage fit, sector alignment, and basic financials
3. **15-minute screening calls** with all 11 shortlisted founders, conducted by Daniel Reeves and Ava Lindström across October 20–31
4. **Final selection**: 5 startups invited to pitch. 2 runners-up notified as alternates.

The 6 rejected after calls were sent personalized rejection emails with specific feedback. One founder (NeoCraft) asked for a follow-up; Daniel offered a 30-minute advisory call in January.

## Investment Parameters

- **Check size**: €100K – €400K per startup
- **Total budget for Demo Day Alpha**: €600K allocated from Fund I
- **Target ownership**: 5–12% equity post-money
- **Stage**: Pre-seed to early seed
- **Sectors**: AI/ML applications, B2B SaaS, marketplace models, regulatory technology, sustainability-adjacent tech
- **Geography**: Italy-based or Italy-connected founding teams

## Evaluation Framework

The IC will use a weighted scoring matrix (detailed in separate document) covering Team (25%), Market (20%), Product (20%), Traction (20%), and Strategic Fit (15%). Minimum threshold: 3.2/5.0 weighted average.

Decisions will be classified as: **INVEST**, **CONDITIONAL** (with specific milestones), or **PASS**.

## Expected Outcomes

We anticipate making 2–3 investments from this cohort. The budget allows for one larger check (€300K+) if a standout candidate emerges, or three smaller checks in the €150K–€200K range.

Post-event, Tomás will prepare full investment dossiers for each startup within 10 business days. Dossiers will cover: executive summary, team assessment, product/tech analysis, market sizing, competitive landscape, financial review, risk matrix, and IC recommendation.

## Logistics

- **Venue**: Palazzo delle Industrie, Sala Colonne — capacity 80, confirmed for 17:00–22:00
- **Expected attendance**: 25–30 (IC + Vertex Minds team + startup delegations + 8–10 invited guests from our LP network)
- **AV setup**: projector, clicker, timer display for founders. Tomás coordinating with venue AV team.
- **Catering**: aperitif provided by venue, budget €1,200
