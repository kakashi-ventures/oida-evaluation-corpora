# Caso C — Vertex Minds Investment Committee

**Domain:** Venture Capital / Investment Decision-Making

## Scenario

Vertex Minds LLC is an early-stage VC fund based in Milan, Italy, organizing its inaugural Demo Day Alpha. Five startups are selected from a pipeline of 30 applications to pitch to the investment committee. The corpus covers the full investment lifecycle: sourcing, screening, pitch day, IC deliberation, and final allocation decisions.

Investment parameters: EUR 100K–400K check size, 5–12% equity post-money, pre-seed to early seed stage, with EUR 600K total budget allocated from Fund I.

## Epistemic Characteristics

This corpus is designed to contain:

- **High-stakes decisions** with explicit rationale and dissenting opinions
- **Evaluations** of startups from multiple IC members with divergent assessments
- **Contradictions** between screening scores and pitch-day impressions
- **Questions** about due diligence items that remain open during deliberation
- **Temporal density** — daily calendar snapshots spanning the evaluation period
