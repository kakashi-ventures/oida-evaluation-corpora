# Term Sheet Draft: NovaTech AI

**INVESTMENT MEMORANDUM**

**Date:** November 15, 2025
**Investor:** Vertex Minds LLC (Daniel Reeves, CEO; Ava Lindström, Co-IC)
**Company:** NovaTech AI
**Founders:** Marco Bianchi (CEO), Yara Osman (CTO)

---

## 1. Investment Terms

| Item | Terms |
|------|-------|
| **Investment Amount** | €200,000 |
| **Instrument** | SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) |
| **Valuation Cap** | €2.3M (pre-money equivalent) |
| **Discount Rate** | 20% to next priced round |
| **Pro-rata Rights** | Yes, for funding rounds up to and including Series A |

### 1.1 Investment Mechanics
Vertex Minds commits to invest €200,000 in NovaTech AI via a SAFE instrument, without creating immediate equity or debt obligations. Upon a qualifying priced round (Series Seed or beyond) or liquidation event, the SAFE will convert at the valuation cap or with the discount rate applied, whichever is more favorable to Vertex Minds.

---

## 2. Governance & Rights

### 2.1 Board Observation
Vertex Minds shall have the right to designate one observer to attend all meetings of the Board of Directors, with information rights equivalent to board members but without voting privileges.

### 2.2 Information Rights
- **Monthly Financial Reporting:** NovaTech AI shall provide monthly management accounts (P&L, balance sheet, cash flow statement) no later than the 10th of the following month.
- **Quarterly Board Updates:** Quarterly written updates covering operational metrics, product development milestones, customer acquisition, and burn rate projections.
- **Annual Audited Financials:** Audited financial statements upon request or as required by law.

### 2.3 Access & Inspection
Vertex Minds retains the right to inspect the books and records of NovaTech AI upon reasonable notice (minimum 5 business days).

---

## 3. Founder Commitments

### 3.1 Vesting Schedule
Both Marco Bianchi and Yara Osman are subject to the following vesting schedule:
- **Cliff:** 1 year
- **Total Vesting Period:** 4 years
- **Vesting Type:** Monthly vesting following the cliff

Unvested shares shall be subject to acceleration only upon a change of control or mutual written agreement.

### 3.2 Key Person Clause
The continued employment of both Marco Bianchi and Yara Osman is material to this investment. In the event either founder departs for any reason (voluntary or involuntary), Vertex Minds reserves the right to liquidate its position at fair market value or negotiate alternative terms, to be determined within 30 days of departure.

### 3.3 Non-Compete Obligation
Each founder agrees to a non-compete covenant for a period of 24 months following voluntary departure from NovaTech AI. The scope is limited to direct competitors in the AI-powered enterprise automation space.

---

## 4. Conditions Precedent

This investment is conditional upon the following being satisfied to Vertex Minds' reasonable satisfaction:

1. **Reference Check – CTO:** Vertex Minds shall conduct a background and professional reference check on Yara Osman, the CTO. This check shall cover prior employment, technical contributions, and any relevant professional disputes or IP claims.

2. **IP Ownership Verification:** NovaTech AI shall provide satisfactory evidence that all material intellectual property has been properly assigned from the founders to the company, with no third-party claims or encumbrances.

---

## 5. Exclusivity & Closing

### 5.1 Exclusivity Period
From the date this term sheet is signed, NovaTech AI shall be exclusive to Vertex Minds for a period of **30 days** for the purposes of this investment round. During this period, NovaTech AI shall not solicit, encourage, or engage in discussions regarding comparable financing from other parties.

### 5.2 Closing
Closing shall occur within 15 business days of satisfaction of all conditions precedent, or such other date as mutually agreed in writing.

---

## 6. Standard SAFE Terms

This investment shall be governed by the standard Andreessen Horowitz (a16z) SAFE template, with modifications as follows:
- MFN (Most Favored Nation) clause applies to any SAFE subsequently issued at less favorable terms.
- Pro-rata rights shall extend through Series A only; subsequent rounds do not trigger pro-rata rights unless separately negotiated.

---

## 7. Miscellaneous

- **Governing Law:** This term sheet shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the European Union (SAFE framework) and applicable German law where local enforcement is required.
- **Confidentiality:** All parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of the terms herein, except as required by law or to advisors under NDA.
- **Entire Agreement:** This term sheet represents the preliminary agreement and intent; the final SAFE document shall supersede this term sheet upon execution.
