# Term Sheet Draft: UrbanFlow

**INVESTMENT MEMORANDUM**

**Date:** November 18, 2025
**Investor:** Vertex Minds LLC (Daniel Reeves, CEO; Ava Lindström, Co-IC)
**Company:** UrbanFlow (Mobility Systems)
**Founders:** Diana Rossi (CEO), Karim Haddad (Chief Technology Officer)

---

## 1. Investment Terms

| Item | Terms |
|------|-------|
| **Investment Amount** | €180,000 |
| **Original Ask** | €250,000 (negotiated down) |
| **Instrument** | SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) |
| **Valuation Cap** | €1.8M (pre-money equivalent) |
| **Discount Rate** | 20% to next priced round |
| **Pro-rata Rights** | Yes, for all future equity rounds |

### 1.1 Investment Allocation
Vertex Minds commits €180,000 (down from the original €250,000 request) reflecting (a) a more conservative runway estimate, (b) a phased market entry strategy, and (c) mutual agreement on burn rate assumptions. The investment structure follows a standard SAFE instrument with the terms specified herein.

### 1.2 Valuation Rationale
The €1.8M valuation cap reflects UrbanFlow's current stage (pre-launch), team composition, patent status, and comparable company analysis in the smart mobility sector.

---

## 2. Governance & Rights

### 2.1 Board Observation Rights
Vertex Minds shall have the right to appoint one observer to attend all meetings of the Board of Directors. The observer shall receive all board materials in advance of meetings and may participate in discussions, but shall have no voting rights.

### 2.2 Information Rights
- **Monthly Financial Reporting:** UrbanFlow shall provide monthly management accounts (income statement, balance sheet, cash flow, and runway projection) within 10 business days of month-end.
- **Quarterly Updates:** Quarterly written or video updates on operational metrics, hub development status, regulatory milestones, and burn rate.
- **Annual Financials:** Upon request or as required by law.

### 2.3 Inspection & Access
Vertex Minds retains standard rights to inspect books and records of UrbanFlow upon reasonable notice.

---

## 3. Founder Commitments

### 3.1 Vesting Schedule
Diana Rossi and Karim Haddad are each subject to:
- **Cliff:** 1 year
- **Total Period:** 4 years
- **Mechanics:** Monthly vesting following the cliff, with double-trigger acceleration upon change of control

### 3.2 Key Person Clause
The continued employment of both Diana Rossi (CEO) and Karim Haddad (CTO) is material to this investment. Voluntary departure by either founder triggers a 30-day renegotiation period in which Vertex Minds may (a) negotiate alternative terms, (b) liquidate its position at fair market value, or (c) accept the new team structure.

### 3.3 Non-Compete Obligation
Each founder is bound by a 24-month non-compete covenant following voluntary departure from UrbanFlow, limited to the urban micro-mobility and shared transportation sectors.

---

## 4. Milestone Clause & Follow-On Investment Option

### 4.1 Follow-On Participation Right
Vertex Minds reserves the right to participate in a follow-on investment of **up to €150,000** on the **same terms** (same SAFE valuation cap and discount), contingent upon UrbanFlow achieving the following milestone:

**Operational launch of a second hub (geographic market) within 9 months of closing.**

### 4.2 Exercise of Right
- Vertex Minds shall notify UrbanFlow in writing within 15 days of milestone achievement of its intention to participate in the follow-on round.
- The follow-on investment shall close within 30 days of notification, subject to standard conditions precedent.
- If UrbanFlow does not achieve the milestone within the 9-month window, the follow-on right expires automatically.

### 4.3 Rationale for Milestone Clause
UrbanFlow is pursuing a phased hub expansion strategy. This clause incentivizes rapid market validation while allowing Vertex Minds to increase conviction through observable operational progress before deploying additional capital.

---

## 5. Conditions Precedent

This investment is conditional upon satisfaction of the following:

1. **Patent Application Filing Confirmation:** UrbanFlow shall provide evidence that a patent application for its proprietary routing algorithm has been filed with the appropriate patent office (EPO or equivalent). Filing must occur on or before closing.

2. **IP Assignment Agreement:** All founders shall execute a comprehensive IP assignment agreement assigning all past, present, and future intellectual property (inventions, software, documentation, know-how) to UrbanFlow. No exceptions or holdbacks without prior written consent of Vertex Minds.

---

## 6. Exclusivity Period

From the execution of this term sheet, UrbanFlow shall grant Vertex Minds an **exclusive 30-day period** to complete its due diligence and negotiate final SAFE terms. During this period, UrbanFlow shall not solicit or engage in substantive discussions with competing investors regarding comparable financing.

---

## 7. SAFE Framework

This investment shall follow the standard a16z SAFE template with the following modifications:
- **MFN Clause:** Applies to any SAFE issued at more favorable terms within 12 months of this investment.
- **Pro-rata Extension:** Pro-rata rights extend through subsequent equity rounds (Series Seed, Series A, Series B) unless waived by mutual written agreement.
- **Standard Conversion:** Conversion occurs upon a priced equity round (Series Seed or later) or upon liquidation of UrbanFlow.

---

## 8. Miscellaneous

- **Governing Law:** The SAFE and related agreements shall be governed by the laws of the European Union with respect to investment instruments, and by applicable German or jurisdictional law where UrbanFlow is incorporated.
- **Confidentiality:** All parties agree to maintain confidentiality regarding the terms, except as required by law, professional advisors (under NDA), or board/investor reporting.
- **Entire Agreement:** This term sheet represents the parties' preliminary agreement in principle. Final binding effect occurs only upon execution of the formal SAFE document.
- **Amendments:** Any modification to this term sheet must be in writing and signed by both parties.
