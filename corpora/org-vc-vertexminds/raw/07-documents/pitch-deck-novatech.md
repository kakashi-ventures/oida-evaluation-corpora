## Slide 2: The Problem
Architecture and construction firms spend 40–60% of project time on documentation:
- Design revisions require manual updates across 15+ linked documents
- Regulatory compliance demands create 200+ page specifications
- Client communication generates redundant PDFs, CAD exports, and compliance checklists
- Current tools: fragmented (CAD software, Word, email)

**Pain:** €12–18K wasted per medium project. 3 projects/quarter per firm.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
NovaTech AI automates document generation and synchronization:
- AI reads project parameters (dimensions, materials, codes) from CAD/BIM models
- Auto-generates compliant specs, drawings lists, and client reports
- Real-time sync: design change → all documents update in minutes
- Integrates with Revit, ArchiCAD, AutoCAD
- Outputs: PDFs, Word docs, compliance checklists
